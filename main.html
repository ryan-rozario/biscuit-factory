Biscuit sales at and all time high

Despite efforts to of regulators to clamp down on the sale of higher-sugar goods, The Great Indian biscuit factory is producing more biscuits than ever. Biscuits have seen an increase growth of sales by 4% compared to last quarter. People need biscuits and efforts by the regulatory bodies to reduce consumption has failed. Titanium Financials the primary investor in the Great Indian Biscuits is pleased with the outcomes.
